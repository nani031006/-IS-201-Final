New Zealand

New Zealand is a gorgeous country. Outside of Auckland, the countryside is amazing but the roads to get anywhere are hard to navigate and the average speed limit is 50 miles per hours. My family is from there so this is the country that I have visited the most. It is my favorite place on earth and the food is so good. Mincemeat pies, fish and chips, and the best butter chicken outside of India.

Taupo

Taupo is a town located in the central North Island of New Zealand, known for its stunning lake and geothermal activity. Lake Taupo is the largest lake in New Zealand and the Southern Hemisphere and offers various recreational activities such as fishing, boating, and swimming. The town is also famous for its hot springs, geysers, and mud pools, making it a popular destination for relaxation and adventure. Debrets Hot Pools is the best hot pools to go but it is pricey now due to tourism. Taupo is surrounded by beautiful landscapes, including mountains and forests, providing opportunities for hiking and outdoor exploration.

Auckland

Auckland is a vibrant city located on the North Island of New Zealand. It is known for its stunning harbor, beautiful beaches, and diverse culture. Auckland is the largest city in New Zealand and serves as a major economic and cultural hub. The city is surrounded by water, with numerous islands and beaches to explore. Auckland is also home to iconic landmarks such as the Sky Tower, which offers panoramic views of the city and beyond. The city has a thriving arts scene, with galleries, theaters, and music venues showcasing local talent. Auckland is a great base for exploring the natural beauty of New Zealand.

Rotorua

Rotorua is a smelly town. It is famous for its geothermal activity, including bubbling mud pools, steaming vents, and geysers. The smell comes from the sulfur in the geothermal areas, which gives the town its distinctive aroma. Despite the smell, Rotorua is a popular tourist destination, offering unique cultural experiences such as traditional Maori performances and hangi feasts. Visitors can also enjoy relaxing in natural hot springs and exploring the beautiful surrounding forests and lakes.
